About John

J.E. (John) McDonald is a storyteller who allows his heart and imagination to lead the way, and most of the time, he doesn't even know how his stories will end.

John grew up in Louisville, Kentucky with his parents and three brothers. He moved to the Dallas-Fort Worth area during his high school years and resides in that area still today. Some of his fondest memories are of his mom telling him bedtime stories. John has been telling and writing stories most of his life, and with the encouragement of family and friends, he took the next step into book writing. His highest hope is that you will find some joy in reading his books.
